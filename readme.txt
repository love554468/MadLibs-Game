What is a mad libs?

According to wikipedia, mad libs is a phrasal template word game,  usually played at
parties, where one player prompts other players for a list of words to substitute
blanks in a story before reading out the completed story aloud

new word:
phrasal: cụm từ
template: mẫu
prompt: lời nhắc
substitute: replace
blank: chỗ trống
aloud: lớn tiếng
